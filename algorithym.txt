Laboratory Activity 2: Determine if a student Passed or Failed

Step 1: Start.
Step 2: Ask the user to enter the grade. Store it as grade.
Step 3: Check if grade is 60 or above.
Step 4: If yes, display "Passed".
Step 5: If no, display "Failed".
Step 6: End.

Step 1: Start.
